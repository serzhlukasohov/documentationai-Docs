## Create Your Account

Sign up for Serhii Lukashov in just a few steps. This grants you access to unlimited documentation projects.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email and create a strong password. Use a real email like `you@example.com` for verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox and click the verification link. This enables project creation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer a quick survey about your use case (documentation, notes, or knowledge base).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication immediately after signup for added security.
</Callout>

## Create a New Project

Once logged in, set up your first documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Project" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select **New Project**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Project" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like `My First Docs` and choose a slug (e.g., `my-first-docs`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Template" icon="file-text">
    Pick the **Blank Documentation** template for a clean start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Click **Create**. Your project is now live at `https://docs.example.com/my-first-docs`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Document

Add content using the visual editor or MDX. Choose your preferred method below.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Visual Editor" icon="edit">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="New Page">
        Click **+ New Page** in your project sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Edit Content">
        Type your title and content. Use the toolbar for headings, lists, and embeds.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish">
        Hit **Publish** to make it live.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="MDX Editor" icon="code">
    Write raw MDX for full control.

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Preview">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Welcome
    description: Your first page.
    ---

    ## Hello World

    This is your first MDX document in Serhii Lukashov.
    ````
    ````markdown
    # Preview (Rendered)

    ## Hello World

    This is your first MDX document in Serhii Lukashov.
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
