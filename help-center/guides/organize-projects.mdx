---
title: Organizing Your Documentation Projects
description: Quick steps to structure your docs efficiently.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click "New Project" in your dashboard and name it after your topic or app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add folders">
    Use folders to group related documentation (e.g., Guides, API, Tutorials).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add pages">
    Within each folder, add pages for individual guides or features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Use the "Share" button to add teammates for collaborative editing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
